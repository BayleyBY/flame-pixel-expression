# matte_invert

**What it does:** Inverts the matte (`1 - m1`).

**Use case:** Flip a matte's inside/outside.

**Inputs:** Matte 1

**Expects:** any (data / value op)

_No variables._

## Notes

Inverts the matte (`1 - m1`) — flips inside and outside.

### Quick test
Instant with just **Matte 1**: white ↔ black flips the moment it loads (view Result; a
solid-alpha clip turns black).
