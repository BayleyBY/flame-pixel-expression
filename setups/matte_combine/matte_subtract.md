# matte_subtract

**What it does:** Matte 1 minus Matte 2 (clamped).

**Use case:** Cut one matte out of another (holdouts).

**Inputs:** Matte 1 + Matte 2

**Expects:** any (data / value op)

_No variables._

## Notes

Matte 1 minus Matte 2, clamped to 0..1 — cuts Matte 2 out of Matte 1 (holdouts). Inputs on
Matte 1 and Matte 2.
